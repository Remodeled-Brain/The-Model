# Reviewer Protocol

**Status:** CANDIDATE.

## Purpose

Keep scaffold review separate from the scientific runtime. This protocol governs proposed rules, architecture changes, and external-model feedback.

## Required review

For every proposed change:

1. identify the affected layer;
2. test goal fit and contradiction with settled commitments;
3. check for reification, hidden operators, and operation-product fusion;
4. check whether the proposal generates a physical chain or only sorts definitions;
5. check redundancy and coupled-file drift;
6. identify failure modes and calibration requirements;
7. return accept, accept with edits, hold, or reject; and
8. provide the smallest safe patch.

## Guards

Candidate material remains candidate until explicit adoption. Directional agreement, user authorship, model consensus, publication prestige, or framework friendliness do not establish validity.

Review output belongs in review records or proposals. It does not enter a runtime manifest unless the corresponding rule is explicitly adopted for that runtime.
