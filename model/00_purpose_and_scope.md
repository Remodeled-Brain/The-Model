# The Model — Purpose and Scope

**Status:** CANDIDATE architectural refactor.

## Primary purpose

The Model determines what construct, target relation, and causal burden a question actually contains. It then admits evidence from the measured data rather than the published narrative and reconstructs only the physical relations that survive construct, design, counterfactual, bridge, and chain checks.

The shared rules are domain-neutral:

1. reject reified labels and hidden operators that supply causal content by name;
2. classify the exact target relation before assigning causal status;
3. extract the actual observations, comparator, effect distribution, heterogeneity, attrition, durability, measurement validity, and analysis limits;
4. treat every increase from observation to construct, direction, mechanism, effectiveness, named process, or cross-scale meaning as a separate inferential bridge;
5. begin observational evidence at descriptive or more restrictive scope;
6. allow intervention evidence to earn causality for the exact manipulated relation when its full data and counterfactual burden closes;
7. require separate bridges for etiology, endogenous mechanism, construct validity, and cross-scale explanation;
8. bind evidence by independent family and exact chain contribution rather than paper count; and
9. return the strongest data-supported answer without restoring failed narrative claims.

Construct dependence and measurement distance remain independent restrictions. Construct-indexed evidence begins `label_dependent`. Proxy or surrogate evidence begins `proxy_limited`. Study design cannot erase those restrictions by itself.

## Operating chain

`source question -> construct admission -> target-relation classification -> evidence design and data extraction -> inferential-bridge audit -> causal admission by target -> physical reconstruction -> route discrimination -> eligible evidence binding -> bounded answer`

A failed broad claim does not erase a valid narrower observation or intervention relation. A valid intervention relation does not automatically close the untreated system's origin, endogenous route, or full mechanism.

## Generic and domain-specific layers

The kernel and primary runtime contain only general mechanics. Domain cartridges supply specialized handle instances, known inference failures, translation burdens, and regression cases.

The neuroscience cartridge therefore owns examples involving diagnostic categories, named brain regions, psychiatric treatment claims, rating scales, imaging, biomarkers, stimulation, and named processes such as “neuroplasticity.” The generic runtime must remain usable outside neuroscience without inheriting those concrete examples as universal rules.

## Evidence admission

### Observational evidence

Observational evidence may fully support a descriptive relation. Causal promotion requires affirmative closure of the burden appropriate to the claimed target, including temporal direction, counterfactual adequacy, alternative-route testing, practical magnitude, stable conditions, independence, and every claimed mechanism or scale bridge. Adjustment, recurrence, large samples, or causal vocabulary cannot substitute for those dependencies.

### Intervention evidence

Intervention evidence is evaluated from manipulation integrity, comparator, timing, measurement validity, full response distribution, practical magnitude, heterogeneity, specificity, durability, attrition, adverse outcomes, replication, and disconfirmation.

A decisive controlled intervention may establish intervention-to-outcome causality. Treatment or intervention status neither grants nor denies that result. Broader claims about etiology, endogenous mechanism, construct validity, or cross-scale explanation remain separate targets.

### Narrative discipline

The actual data outrank the abstract, discussion, conclusion, guideline, review, or consensus narrative. Terms such as “effective,” “treats,” “normalizes,” “induces,” or “mechanistic” may appear only when the data support their exact target, comparator, practical, temporal, and physical scope.

## Confidence

Confidence remains attached to the operation that generated it:

- **compilation confidence:** security of the mapping from the source question to physical variables and target relations;
- **evidence-admission confidence:** completeness and validity of the extracted data and inferential bridges;
- **causal-target status:** whether the exact target relation passes its design-specific causal burden;
- **segment confidence:** closure of each required chain segment and join;
- **scale-translation confidence:** closure of the physical path between measured and requested scales;
- **answer-translation confidence:** how directly the closed relation answers the source wording.

## Non-goals

The Model does not:

- treat labels, regions, faculties, therapies, or named processes as causal objects by name;
- treat observational recurrence as causal closure;
- reject a decisive intervention merely because ordinary evidence in the field is weak;
- infer etiology or endogenous mechanism from intervention response alone;
- translate statistical significance into effectiveness;
- hide response heterogeneity behind a group mean;
- rename a proxy or pre-post measurement as a biological process;
- allow publication volume, prestige, peer review, consensus, or meta-analysis to repair invalid inputs;
- erase valid narrow observations when broader claims fail; or
- allow domain-specific assumptions to leak silently into the generic kernel.
