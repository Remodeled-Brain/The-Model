# Ingest Support Runtime

**Status:** CANDIDATE.

## Role

Ingest maintains the evidence substrate used by the question runtime. It extracts the actual data, classifies evidence design and target relation, separates observations from narrative, audits inferential bridges, fingerprints the generators shared across sources, assigns relation-specific evidence families, and identifies the independent constraint paths that survive family collapse. It does not define the user’s question or transfer a source conclusion into the final answer.

## Required boundary

Ingest may update available observations, data-supported scope, evidence-family membership, bridge closure, supersession status, chain-segment closure, disconfirmation, target-specific confidence, and relation-specific repair requirements. It may not silently change the chain kernel, question-compilation grammar, domain cartridge, or settled claim layer. A proposed repair is an instruction for future evidence and never changes the current admission state.

## Operating sequence

`source -> identity resolution -> data extraction -> design and target classification -> generator fingerprint -> narrative stripping -> bridge audit -> relation-specific validation -> family assignment -> family collapse when triggered -> repair ledger -> support record -> evidence binder`

Every source receives a generator fingerprint. Full family collapse runs for synthesis, promotion, claimed replication or convergence, rapid literature expansion, and any target relation supported by three or more sources. The unit of accumulated weight is the independent constraint path rather than the paper. Recurrence within one family may improve precision, map heterogeneity, or test operation stability, but it cannot supply independent closure for the generator it repeats.

Observational evidence begins descriptive or more restricted. Intervention evidence may earn causality for the exact manipulated relation when the comparator, effect distribution, heterogeneity, durability, missingness, measurement, replication, and disconfirmation burden closes. Neither evidence class receives causal status by name.

## Repair output

Every failed gate, unresolved load-bearing bridge, promotion block, or synthesis-level shared dependency emits a bounded repair instruction. The instruction states the exact missing dependency, why the current generator cannot close it, the minimal measurement or perturbation required, the comparator, the needed independence from existing families, the passing observation, the disconfirming observation, and the scope that would remain limited after a pass. Generic requests for more research are invalid.

## Recency

Recent evidence receives retrieval and supersession priority. Recency does not provide causal weight. A newer source supersedes an older relation only when it tests the same target under compatible conditions with stronger, more direct, or disconfirming evidence.

## Output

The ingest runtime emits audited relation-level support records, generator-family graphs when triggered, independent-constraint-path summaries, and operational repair ledgers. Canonical integration remains serialized behind explicit adoption.
